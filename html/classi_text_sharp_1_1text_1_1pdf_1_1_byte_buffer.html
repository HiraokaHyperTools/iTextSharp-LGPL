Detailed Description

Acts like a StringBuilder but works with byte arrays. floating point is converted to a format suitable to the PDF.

Author
Paulo Soares (psoar.nosp@m.es@c.nosp@m.onsis.nosp@m.te.p.nosp@m.t)
